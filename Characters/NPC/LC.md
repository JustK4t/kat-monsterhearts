**Bio**
LC moved to town a few years ago when their parents got jobs at [[The Lab]]. From the start they have been.. very vocal about how they feel about this town and its inhabitants, as well as their own parents part in the military industrial complex. Harsh, icy, and intimidatingly cool looking they haven't made many close friends which is fine because no one gets it. LC plays all the instruments in a punk band but wont allow anyone else to join. The name of the band changes too frequently to track. One of the only kids who smokes and has a fake ID they often purchase liquor and are invited to nearly every party as a result.

They are a good student, failing everything as a protest, don't have any close friends, and have a distant relationship with two parents that love them very much by choice.
