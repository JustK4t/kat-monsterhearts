    An interview with Alyssa Merrymont

    The Gazette, September edition, 2004
    Roll Call: The Girl who Appeared Out of Nowhere!
    (Written and Edited by Trish Gold)

    School is in session, and the summer heat blew in a new wave of classmates! Here at the Gazette, we wanted to try something new this year and tell you about some the people who you might see in the halls! Our first highlight is on the mysterious junior Alyssa Merrymont! Nobody seems to know much about her, nobody saw her in town until a few months ago. Her fashion sense is super plain, but if you get a look at her face, you'll see she's actually super pretty! I think she had a few bad hair days, maybe that's why she likes wearing hats (even if the teachers tell her to take them off). I managed to sit down with her at lunch and ask a few questions (it only took me a few days of asking to finally get her to say yes)!

    Why did you come to Maple Valley High? "It was the closest to my home, and I was not going to that Galoron forsaken Rival School" (Anyone know who Galoron is?)

    What are you most excited for this school yeah? "I am hoping the weather stays fai- ... good for the rest of the year, so I may practice my swordsmanship outside." (She knows how to use a sword? People still do that?? Watch out boys!)

    What item do you HAVE to have when you leave the house? "I like my cell phone, I was surprised at how much fun I found myself having while using it." (It's a R*zr, you know she's cool!)

    So far, her classmates say she's really hard to talk to, and doesn't seem to have a ton of friends, so if you see her in the halls, be sure to say hi! Some of them also said that their name reminded them of a character in a game, so maybe you can talk to her about that! (Just look at her picture, who wouldn't want to be friends with her!) That's all the space we have for this month, we'll make sure to introduce another awesome classmate in next month's edition of Roll Call!
