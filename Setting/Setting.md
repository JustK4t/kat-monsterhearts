[[The Town]] is a small town set in the heart of Appalachia, deep in the forests of southern West Virginia close to the Virginia border. Initially sustained by logging and mining industry the town declined until the creation of [[The Lab]] in the 60s which brought high paying jobs and new faces to town. Life is slower here. One public school, one private. 25 minutes on mountain roads to the highway, then another 15 to the nearest mall.

Our story begins in September, 2004. A new school year at [[The School]] has brought fresh faces and drug buried secrets closer than ever to the surface. The overall world is not aware of monsters and would not react well, the veil must be maintained.

Our main characters are: [[Aliya Raventhorne]], [[Alyssa Merrymont]], [[Annalise Devin]], [[Everette Eerie]], and [[Graye Wilde]].
