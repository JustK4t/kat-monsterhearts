# **Alyssa Merrymont**{: .bio-page-title}

## **Bio**{: .bio-section-title}

Alyssa Merrymont is the girl who seems to have "just shown up" in Oak Vale this summer. She seems reserved and cold, and when you do get to speak with her, you learn that she doesn't exactly talk like someone from the area. When you look at her, you can't quite tell the style she's trying to pull off, is it a weird kind of fashion? Trying to be a tomboy? Alyssa always seems to be a bit distant, and even when she's engaged in what she's doing, you can almost get the sense she feels as though she'll disappear in the next moment.

The [Winter Springs Motel](Winter Springs Motel.md) is graciously housing her, and the owner has come to refer to her as the "Queen of the Motel", a title which she doesn't seem to mind. If you visit the Motel, you may see Alyssa practicing swordplay with a real, honest-to-goodness sword, and you would see her tired green eyes start to sparkle in excitement. 

Oak Vale still might not know where she came from or why she's staying, but her presence is sure to turn some heads.

## **Homeroom questions**
**Who do you have a crush on? How long and how bad is it? (This can be more than one person esp if NPCs)**
Alyssa has come down with a bad case of "crush on Melody Evermore". The two met around a month after Alyssa "arrived" in town, Alyssa kept hearing people talk about how good the "[Oh Deer Cafe](Oh Deer Cafe.md)" was so she just had to check it out. The first time she went, Alyssa thought that Melody was a bit cute, but she found herself continuing to come back, and shortly after that realized that her affection was beginning to go deeper, and a mixture of feelings pushed her to stop going. She thought that she would be 'freed' from these feelings if she didn't visit the Cafe anymore, yet look who she got seated next to in Homeroom...
Alyssa also has found herself slightly drawn in by the allure of "Amy". For different reasons than with Melody though, she feels a hesitation to pursue these feelings further, but she can't quite put her finger on why...

**Who does that person have a crush on?**
When Melody was young, she spent alot of time around a certain girl, and whenever they were together, a warmth she couldn't describe filled her chest. The two have been apart for a number of years now, and since then, she's never felt that same feeling. Since then, she has come to follow in the footsteps of that friend and become quite flirty. Part of it is for fun, and the other part is that she longs to feel that warmth again. In searching for that feeling for so many years though, her original purpose has become somewhat obfuscated to the point that she barely remember it in the first place.

**Lance seems to be hanging around the winter springs motel more doing odd jobs for Willy, how does it feel naturally accruing knight coded people around you? Does it feel like you’re forging your own path or just falling back into a mold?**
Being a princess is and was a core to who Alyssa is, but it is also a part of her that she has some loathing for. In recent times, she has come to land more on the loathing side as she tries to run from her past, but seeing how purehearted Lance is about wanting to "be a knight" reminds her that though the position was thrust upon her in her "past life", it still meant something. Her position inspired many, and though she had become jaded, it was not a role she inherently disliked, so Lance may help to unlock some of those feelings once more.

**You keep seeing bad omens from your games around town, but surely since it’s the real world they’re just coincidences. What are the signs and omens?**
For the most part, Alyssa's games were stereotypical RPGs. However, there were a few things they did which were out of the ordinary, and one such thing was showing Alyssa's time at the Royal Academy. While there, its shown that Alyssa went through a deal of bullying due to people either thinking she had no talent of her own, or deriding her for being the person who was going to be the new Princess. Such bullying took the form of tearing her notebooks, stealing her clothes, writing foul names upon her desk. Though 'canonically' that happened years ago, the memories are still fresh in Alyssa's head, and though she wants to think they mean nothing, similar things have begun happening at school. Yes, one could say that these are just typical things bullies do, but the words they choose, the things they do... the resemblance is beginning to grow strong. Various other scenes stick out in Alyssa's head, and a sinking feeling in her stomach begs, prays that those scenes never come to pass. She was free, she wasn't bound by those systems anymore... was she?

**Melody came to school with a black eye last week and won’t talk about it, what the fuck is up with that?**
Were this to happen, the school/class would see a different side of Alyssa. She is by no means emotionless, but Alyssa tends to be fairly temperate, her emotions do not swing too far to their extremes (unless you catch her at a particularly good/bad time). However, if she sees Melody with an injury like that, a rage and worry that had not been seen would shine through. She would be extremely impassioned in asking Melody if she was okay, who caused such a thing, anything she could find, and obviously wanting to do anything she could to fix it. Without a solid response, her behavior would once again turn erratic, as she would begin borderline stalking Melody (for a short time) to try and find anything she could as to the cause. She would question Ms. Evermore for any answers as well, but if all "above board" means fail, she would not hesitate to take matters into her own hands. As a matter of fact, it may be a good time to demonstrate the swordplay she has been learning... purely for intimidation, of course. Out of pure spite, her immediate suspicions fall to Streeter, and if he made one single snide comment, Alyssa would be liable to throw herself at him without any regard for consequence.

**Who in class reminds you of your fictional home?**
Due to Alyssa's game being an RPG, the world is populated with creatures and races of all kinds, not just humans. During her time as a Princess Alyssa did a great deal of research into the various other races in her kingdom, and as such, the non-mortal characters stir something in her head, it makes her feel as if her worlds are colliding. Additionally, Mars reminds Alyssa of someone from her "home" who never once treated her like a princess, and that friendship was one which Alyssa treasured very deeply, so she hopes to rekindle something similar.

**Who in class is trying to teach you how to drive on the weekends?**
Though she is not *opposed* to asking for help, Alyssa still feels somewhat self-conscious about it. LC is the type of person Alyssa has gathered "doesn't care about you or what you do"... and that seems like the perfect person to ask for help on such a task. They will likely have far more fun than they should teaching a very incompetent Alyssa the very-definitely-correct rules of the road in a perfect starter car.

**Which two NPCs did you catch making out under the bleachers at last week’s football game? Why are you still thinking about it a week later?**
The sportsball games of this world were still a bit of a mystery to Alyssa, and what better way to try and get over those mysteries than by trying them out. However, upon going to her first event... she got lost in the labyrinth of hallways and doors that led to the field. When she did eventually find one which led outside, it was not on the correct side of the area, so she had to walk her way down. Out of the corner of her eyes though was a bit of movement, and upon focusing her eyes to the darkness of the evening, she saw Cleo and another male classmate having... a very intimate moment under the bleachers. As soon as she noticed what she was seeing, she walked as quickly in the opposite direction as she could, but a fluttering in her chest stayed, and the image still popped up in her head multiple times in the week that followed. The intimacy of the moment, the closeness with each other, but the thing that stuck out the most to Alyssa... ... it was okay for two people of the same sex to do that in this world?

**Did it hurt when you got hit by a car, fell down a mountain, and got mauled by a bear before dying last week?**
Yes, it was excruciating. Alyssa is hoping very much not to repeat the incident in this version of the canon.
