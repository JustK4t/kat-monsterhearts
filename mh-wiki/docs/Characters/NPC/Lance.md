Kay "Lance" Fairchild
15 (He started kindergarten way early, so he's the youngest in your class)
He/Him

Absolutely OBSESSED with anything medieval. When Lance was young, his parents would read him Arthurian legends as bedtime stories, and his dreams would be filled with the tales of knights and princesses. Though he was named Kay as an homage to his Mom's father, he began to hate the name when he learned of Sir Kay's behavior, and instead wanted to aspire to be like his favorite knight, Sir Lancelot, so he always introduces himself as Lance.

Due to his obsession, Lance acts a bit abonrmally, referring to everyone as Lord and Lady, and thinking of himself as a knight, though with none of the physique to back up such claims. As such, he both finds himself bullied frequently, and also comes to the defense of those who are being bullied, but often ends up just taking the bulling in their stead rather than driving the bullies away.

He has a heart of gold, as he really does want to live up to the reputation of his 'namesake', and be opposite of his actual namesake. He lives on the same street as Trish and they have been friends since childhood - Trish thinks of him as a younger brother and has often come to rescue him from situations. He may still be a bit childish and figuring himself out, but he wears his heart on his sleeve and is always there to help out someone in need, even if they may have less than noble ulterior motives...

(Editor's note: he does not actually have a sword, the photo gallery where yearbook pictures are taken had a sword and he geeked out)
