## Jack Chetly
Jack earned the unfortunate nickname of "Jock Chudly" his freshman year after setting the high school football records for both rushing yards and concussions. He is naturally athletic and the star of any sport that favors brawn. He's pretty chill but can run a little hot sometimes. He spends his non sports free time working on his classic muscle car and working out. He is 6'4" making him the tallest character in the game.

Jack tries in school but is only passing by the grace of the faculty, his parents put a lot of pressure on him to perform and conform to their standards, he has many friends but he worries if they would still like him if he couldn't play sports anymore.
