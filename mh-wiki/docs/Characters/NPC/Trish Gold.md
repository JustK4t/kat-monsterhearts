# **Trish Gold**{: .bio-page-title}

## **Bio**{: .bi-section-title}

Trish has lived in [Oak Vale](Oak Vale.md) her whole life. She's into journalism as a byproduct of her true hobby: being obsessed with the supernatural. Trish is fully mortal and has never actually had a supernatural experience, but is always yammering on about Frogmen in [the Lake](Lake Valor.md) or the Alien hive underneath [Brookhaven](Brookhaven National Laboratory.md). She does her best with her writing for [The School Newspaper](The Gazette.md) and takes it very seriously despite always managing to miss the real point of the story or make the person she is writing about seem really awkward. Junior, decent student, handful of friends, energetic and upbeat even in the face of very direct bullying. Trish manages to dress like a combination librarian and 19th century schoolteacher but makes it work.

She is a decent student, has a close handful of friends, and a good home life.
