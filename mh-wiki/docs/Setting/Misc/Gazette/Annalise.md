[Oak Vale High Gazette](../The%20Gazette.md), September Edition (Part 2), 2004

Roll Call: It's Like Seventh Heaven with our New Friend Devin!
(Written and Edited by [Trish Gold](../../../Characters/NPC/Trish%20Gold.md))

Hey Vultures! I wasn't sure if I was going to write any more articles this month, but I, Trish Gold, had to let everyone know about the AWESOME person I got to meet - [Annalise Devin](../../../Characters/Main/Annalise%20Devin.md)! I was getting ready to leave a few days ago and I saw a girl sitting alone in her classroom and she looked so cool! Her hair totally looks like she hasn't washed it but it is washed and is a really cool color!

People say she can't see super well, but I never saw her run into anything (maybe she has echolocation like a bat)! I also saw someone try to make a joke about her, and she said that they would "lose their dog" next week or something - I don't know what it means, but it's a super cool comeback, I'm going to make it my new slang!

I was able to talk to Annalise as she was about to leave school, and she let me ask some questions! She said that I could only ask about school stuff and not about super personal stuff, which is weird, I never ask about super personal stuff and I DEFINITELY wouldn't write about it! 

Why did you come to our school?
"I mean, I’ve been living here in town since I was three years old, and it just so happens that Oak Vale has better accommodations for me than [neighbouring towns’ schools], so it kind of works out for me. And anyway, it’s where [Lucas](../../../Characters/NPC/Adults.md#lucas-devin) went." (Lucas is her older brother, he sounds awesome!)

Wait, THAT Lucas Devin?? His name is on the Baseball trophy in the lobby! Where is he now??
"He goes to [nearby city college], studying to be a shrink. He still lives here in town though." (Annalise is totally related to our school's royalty!)

Do you do any sports? Do you want to do Baseball like your brother?
"I dabble in the chess club every now and then. And I'm not as athletic as Lucas, but I've been taking part in the [regional] ice skating club as part of the solo ice dance division." (She likes to ice skate, that makes sense, she's like an Ice Queen!)

Annalise did say I smelled 'safe' or something, which I don't know what it means, but my new shampoo must be working! She might look like she doesn't like you at all and doesn't want to talk to you and seems like a total loner, you should definitely talk with her, I had lots of fun! 

That's all for now Vultures, I'll see you in my next article! Until then, don't lose your dog!!
