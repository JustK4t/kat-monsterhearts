[Oak Vale High Gazette](../The%20Gazette.md), August Edition (Part 4), 2004

Roll Call: Give me an A! Give me an M! Give me a Y! What's that spell? Amy!
(Written and Edited by [Trish Gold](../../../Characters/NPC/Trish%20Gold.md))

Hello again Vultures! I hope everyone's adjusting to the new school year! I've been so busy that sometimes I forget to eat lunch, but that's fine because I'm always hungry for a new scoop, and today I've got just the treat for everyone! 

You know how in Geography we learned about all of those other countries and you hoped you could visit them and see how cool they were? Well what if one of those countries came here, and to our school of all place! This year, we have an EXCHANGE STUDENT, isn't that so cool?!? Her name is [Amy McCarthy](../../../Characters/Main/El%20Adair.md), that name is SO COOL!!

When I heard one of our classmates was from Europe this year, I knew I had to get an interview with them, and it turns out Amy and I sit next to each other in Math!! Isn't that crazy?!? It took me a while to get an interview with her, but you know me, I won't let a scoop get away no matter how long it takes! I had to keep asking, but eventually I got a chance to talk to her after gym class after everyone else had gotten changed! I forgot my notebook, but she had one in her bag and let me use it, I like her alot already!! 

She seemed a little distracted because she wasn't fully dressed, but here's what I got to talk to her about!

*Why'd you come to our school?*
“I didn’t really have much of a choice really. Through some friends of family, we had some connections in town and figured it was the best place for us to start putting down roots. I really do love it here though, it’s so *American*… and beautiful too. Kind of like you…" (Who knew people outside of our country were such flatterers!)

*Do you have any fun hobbies outside of school?*
"Well back home I used to be huge into Urban Exploration! I’m also a pretty big collector, I love taking little souvenirs of the interesting places I’ve been." (She must have a huge house to store all of those!)

*What is your home like??*
"Well my home feels pretty far away now, but that might change soon! My family is planning on moving down here but sent me down first as part of this exchange program to… how do you say… “get a lay of the land?” I'm very excited to see them all again!" (I totally must be right, it's a huge house!)

*What's your favorite class?*
"Oh, I absolutely adore drama class! It’s a dream of mine to become a world famous actor! I feel like I even have a shot at it too! I’ve always found I can easily get into the skin of a character, if you know what I mean." (I don't really know what she means, it must be some actor lingo!)


Isn't she so exotic?! She kind of feels like she's hiding some huge secret, but it's probably that she actually likes our school (Vultures stay winning)! She'll probably change alot once she learns all of the cool stuff we have in Oak Vale, so let's make sure that we give her the biggest welcome! Make sure to let her know she's not weird though, Europe seems like a neat country too!

Oh, I forgot! In a first, she also asked me some questions! She asked if I stalked people and if I had fantasies about her, I don't stalk people, I just don't let a good scoop go to waste! And of course I fantasized about her, I had to think of how she'd answer my questions! 

That's all I've got time for, the bell just rang and I wrote through my lunch time again, shoot! Oh well, I'll talk to you all again soon!
