[Oak Vale Gazette](../The%20Gazette.md), August Edition (Part 2), 2004

Roll Call: Former Sports Star Turned Eerie!
(Written and Edited by [Trish Gold](../../../Characters/NPC/Trish%20Gold.md))

Hello again everyone! Trish is back with another profile on one of our outstanding Vultures! [Missy](../../../Characters/NPC/Missy%20Lastname.md) liked my other article enough that she's letting me do two in one month, so you get to hear from your favorite author again! Just kidding, there are a bunch of people way cooler than me, like the person I got to talk to today! 

This person seems cooler than cool, like their skin might be ice cold - Everette Eerie! I think he used to be on our Volleyball team until a year or two ago (he totally looks like he could still hit the ball really well)! 

I scoped him out at lunch for a few days, and he seems like he can seriously eat, I'm surprised he's still so thin! I was finally able to find a chance to talk with him on the edge of the woods when he was leaving school, nobody can say I'm not persistent for a scoop!!

Why'd you choose Oak Vale?
"Uh... it was the closest school to my house. It makes it an easy walk, I didn't want to have to ride the bus every day." (No wonder he's so thin, must be all the walking!)

What's your favorite thing to do when you get home?
"Er... Just watch some TV or something, anything I can do to turn my brain off." (I didn't know you could turn your brain off, I have to learn that skill!)

What's in that journal you always carry around?
"Sorry, that's private, I really don't want anyone else looking in it. And no, it's not like a diary." (It really sounded like a diary, so I had to ask...)

He accidentally dropped his journal while walking away and I got to see a few pages... it seems like it's a book of puzzles or makes or something? I couldn't super tell, but it didn't seem like anything to hide! If you're good at solving puzzles or mysteries, maybe try talking to him! Maybe someone could also buy him some new deodorant, his current one smells a little dead to me! 

Okay, I've got to finish my History paper now, thanks for reading everyone, I'll see you in the next issue of the Gazette!
