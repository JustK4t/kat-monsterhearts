[Oak Vale](Oak Vale.md) is a small mortal town set in the heart of Appalachia, deep in the forests of southern West Virginia close to the Virginia border. Initially sustained by logging and mining industry the town declined until the creation of [Brookhaven National Laboratory](Brookhaven National Laboratory.md) in the late 60s which brought high paying jobs and new faces to town. Life is slower here. 25 minutes on mountain roads to the highway, then another 15 to Roanoke, home of the nearest mall.

Our story begins in September, 2004. A new school year at [Maple Valley High](Maple Valley High.md) has brought fresh faces and pulled buried secrets closer than ever to the surface.

The overall world is not aware of monsters and would not react well, the veil must be maintained.
